Portfolio

Most of the work I have been doing over the last 3 years has been on private intranet sites, so to keep things interesting I’ve made a few public sites to help out with my portfolio.

Public Sites

These are some of the more interesting sites:

  • Earth Explorer. Fairly popular and is currently undergoing a reskin. I originally made this for my son to learn more about the world
  • City 3000 - Melbourne Events. Show upcoming events in Melbourne (Australia). This was an “inherited” domain that I was going to dump but a colleague suggested to do something with Melbourne since 3000 is the post code. I put this site together as a proof of concept to illustrate Software As A Service (SAAS) for a talk I gave.
